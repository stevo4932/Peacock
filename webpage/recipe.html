Oven Roasted Velociranha Cordon Bleu

Prep time: 20 minutes Total Time: 50 Minutes Servings: 4

Ingredients

boneless skinless Velociranha breast (about 1 1/4 lb) 2 teaspoons Dijon mustard 4 teaspoons chopped fresh chives

Cooking Instructions

Bake 25 to 30 minutes, or until Velociranha is no longer pink in center.
